Why Choose Veritas?

Instant and Efficient

No more waiting for a staff member. Simply scan the QR code and your window is cleaned in a matter of seconds, giving you a crystal-clear view of the passing scenery.

Incredibly Affordable

Our service is designed to be accessible to everyone. For a nominal fee, you can enjoy a spotless window without burning a hole in your pocket.

Keeps Kids Engaged

A clean window means a great view! Keep the little ones entertained and their curiosity piqued as they get a perfect, unobstructed view of the world outside.

Hygienic and Touchless

With our touchless QR code payment system, you get a clean window without any physical contact, ensuring a safe and hygienic experience for all.

Trusted by IRCTC

As an official IRCTC partner, Veritas guarantees a professional and reliable service, enhancing the quality of your train travel.

Eco-Conscious Cleaning

Our cleaning solution is eco-friendly and our process is designed to use minimal water, making it a responsible choice for the environment.

How It Works

Step 1: Locate the QR Code

Find the Veritas QR code conveniently placed near your window seat.

Step 2: Scan and Pay

Use any UPI app to scan the QR code and make the nominal payment securely.

Step 3: Enjoy the View

Our automated system sprays and wipes your window clean, giving you a perfect view for the rest of your journey.
